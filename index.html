Short-Term Post Hospitalization Housing (STPHH) Agreement

This agreement facilitates the safe discharge of the Patient/Resident (listed below) from acute care or SNF to a Room and Board facility operated by the subcontractor of Foundation Living.

The Patient/Resident is enrolled in the Anthem Blue Cross Managed Care Program and has been authorized for STPHH services. Authorization is valid for 90 days, with reauthorization required to continue services beyond that period. Total housing support is limited to 180 days from the date of move-in; no extensions will be granted.

Billing & Payment Terms

•   Daily Rate: $66.00

•   Foundation Living will pay the Room and Board provider within 45 days of invoice receipt.

•   If the Patient/Resident leaves and does not return within 24 hours, this agreement ends. This applies even if the Resident is hospitalized or incarcerated. Payment is made through the date of departure only.

Additional Terms

•   Room and Board may not bill the Patient/Resident for services during this agreement.

•   A Field Care Navigator will be assigned to the Patient/Resident.

•   The Patient/Resident must maintain Medi-Cal eligibility and assigned to Anthem Blue Cross for this agreement to remain valid.

Room & Board Requirements

The Room and Board agrees to provide a sober living environment that is clean, safe, and in good repair, with the following amenities:

•   Hot and cold running water; central heating and air conditioning

•   Fully functioning bathroom(s) with toilet, shower/tub, and sink; fully functioning kitchen with refrigerator, freezer, cooktop, microwave, and sink

•   Furnished home with necessary linens and bath towels; availability of basic household cleaning supplies

•   Provisions for three meals per day
